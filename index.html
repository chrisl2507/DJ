About DJ

FROM THE BODYBUILDING STAGE
TO YOUR CORNER.

I'm David-John Stanway — DJ to everyone I train. I spent years as a competitive bodybuilder learning what it takes to transform a physique: discipline, structure and relentless consistency. Now I put that experience to work for you.

These days my focus is performance you can use — getting athletes to the Hyrox start line ready to race, and helping people who manage diabetes train with confidence. Whatever your starting point, the plan is built around you: your schedule, your health, your goals.

RACE DAY

The Hyrox Difference

8 STATIONS. 8 KMS.
ONE RACE PLAN.

Hyrox is the world series of fitness racing: a 1 km run before each of eight functional stations, exactly the same format in every city in the world. It rewards the athlete who trains specifically — and punishes the one who just turns up fit.

  1. 01SkiErg
  2. 02Sled Push
  3. 03Sled Pull
  4. 04Burpee Broad Jumps
  5. 05Rowing
  6. 06Farmers Carry
  7. 07Sandbag Lunges
  8. 08Wall Balls

Whether it's your first race or you're chasing a PB, I'll build your compromised-running engine, sharpen your station efficiency and hand you a pacing plan you can trust when it hurts.
